Problem Description Task. Given two integers a and b, find their least common multiple. 
Input Format. The two integers a and b are given in the same line separated by space. 
Constraints. 1 = a,b = 107.
Output Format. 
Output the least common multiple of a and b.
Sample 1.
Input: 6 8
Output: 24
Among all the positive integers that are divisible by both 6 and 8 (e.g., 48, 480, 24), 24 is the smallest one.
Sample 2. 
Input: 761457 614573
Output: 467970912861
